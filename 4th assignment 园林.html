4th Assignment 中外园林对比 Ver 0.1

中国的园林很有自己的特点。Chinese-style gardens have their distinctive features.

颐和园、苏州园林以及《红楼梦》中的大观园，都和西方园林不同。The Summer Palace, the Classical Gardens of Suzhou and the Grand View Garden described in the distinguished novel Dream of the Red Chamber, none of them are similar to the western-style garden.

像法国凡尔赛等地的园林，一进去，就是笔直的通道，横平竖直，都是几何形的。For example, the garden in Versailles, France is laid out in a formal pattern, aligning horizontally and vertically with straight passages.

中国园林，进门是个大影壁，绕过去，里面遮遮掩掩，曲曲折折，变化多端，走几步就是一番风景，韵味无穷。Different in layout, Chinese-style garden sets a spirit wall at the entrance for shielding. Walking around that wall, there are the crisscross passages inside the garden. Wandering around the labyrinthine corridors, landscape in your sight will change with your forward steps. How atmospheric it is!

在美学思想发展的最初阶段，中国重形象，西方重理性。 In embryo of aesthetic idea, most western artists adopted a rationalism principle, while Chinese artists preferred imagery expressions.

比如庄子的文章，善于利用寓言故事，利用艺术形象来表达他的哲学思想。 For example, Chuang Tze was long at narrating his philosophy through creating artistic characters in allegory stories.

他的思想对后世的文学、绘画等的影响很大。To some certain degree, Chuang Tze’s philosophy have set a tone for Chinese aesthetic development, on literature and painting.

而西方美学是从古希腊传下来的，基本上是一个理性主义的传统，注重规则、规律。In the western countries, they inherited the aesthetic perspective of ancient Greek, which showed rationalism, emphasizing principles and laws.

方方正正的几何体园林也正是这种传统美学思想的体现。 The gardens of geometrical forms in western countries are the reflection of those traditional aesthetic idea.
